YouTube Quizzer

This extension adds a "Quiz" tab in the "In This Video" panel on YouTube.

How to Use:

  1. Go to a YouTube video with captions/subtitles.
  2. Wait a few seconds for the “Quiz” tab to appear under the video.
  3. Click the “Quiz” tab to generate a custom quiz based on the video content.

⚠️ It may take a few seconds to generate your quiz depending on the video length and captions.

Made with ❤️ using Gemini 2.5 Flash
